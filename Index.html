Welcome to the Morija Arts & Cultural Festival

Experience music, culture, and entertainment in the heart of Lesotho!

Event Overview

The Morija Arts and Cultural Festival is one of the biggest cultural events in Lesotho. The festival celebrates Basotho traditions, music, arts, literature, and local food. Every year, thousands of tourists visit Morija to experience traditional dance, poetry, art exhibitions, craft markets, and live music performances. This festival promotes cultural tourism and helps preserve Basotho heritage.

Date and Location

30 September – 1 October 2026
Morija Museum and Cultural Centre, Morija, Lesotho
